# Can Any Company Use This?

## The Short Answer

Yes. This framework is designed to be adapted by any organization, regardless of size, industry, or current AI maturity.

## What "Adapting" Means

This is not a plug-and-play system. It is a structural framework. Adapting it means:

1. **Adopting the principles** — Deciding whether the foundational principles apply to your organization (they generally do)
2. **Applying the layers** — Building your own knowledge architecture, governance model, generation approach, and execution integration
3. **Designing your agents** — Using the common contract and templates to design agents specific to your context
4. **Filling in the knowledge** — Creating and maintaining the organizational knowledge that your agents will rely on

## What You Will Need to Provide

This framework provides the structure. You provide the substance:

| Framework Provides | You Provide |
|-------------------|------------|
| Knowledge architecture model | Your actual organizational knowledge |
| Agent governance structure | Your governance decisions and ownership assignments |
| Agent design templates | Your specific agent purposes and scope definitions |
| Example patterns | Your real business rules and processes |
| Integration layer model | Your actual M365 or other platform configuration |

## Prerequisites

The framework works best for organizations that have (or are willing to build):

- **A commitment to structured knowledge** — Someone must own and maintain the knowledge base
- **Governance willingness** — Leadership must support the idea of governed agents with defined owners
- **Human review culture** — The framework assumes humans review agent outputs before they are acted on

If an organization wants to deploy agents without structure, governance, or human review, this framework will feel like overhead. It is designed for organizations that want **reliability at scale**, not just quick wins.

## Scale Considerations

| Organization Size | Adaptation Approach |
|------------------|-------------------|
| **Small team (< 50)** | Start with 1–2 agents, light governance, a simple knowledge base |
| **Mid-market (50–500)** | Define governance tiers, assign domain owners, build knowledge incrementally |
| **Enterprise (500+)** | Full governance model, multiple knowledge domains, centralized agent registry |

The principles scale. The complexity of implementation scales with the organization.

## Industries

The framework has been applied in professional services contexts. The principles are relevant across:

- Financial services
- Healthcare and life sciences
- Technology and software
- Retail and consumer goods
- Government and public sector
- Education and research

Domain-specific compliance requirements (HIPAA, GDPR, SOX, etc.) should be incorporated into the governance layer and knowledge architecture. This framework does not provide compliance guidance — that is your organization's responsibility.
